Our Story

How we got here

ZAK Medical & Dental Care was started with a simple idea: quality healthcare doesn't need to feel clinical and cold. It should feel personal — like visiting someone who genuinely cares about your wellbeing.

Getting Started

Opened our doors in Sector D, DHA Phase II with a focus on general medicine and dental care.

Growing Our Services

Expanded to cover a full range of dental treatments — from fillings to implants — plus gynecology and aesthetic consultations.

Building Trust

Became a familiar, trusted name for families across DHA Phase II, one appointment at a time.
